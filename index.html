Who I Am

I'm a DevSecOps engineer who has spent 8+ years making security a first-class citizen in software delivery — not an afterthought. My work spans secure coding practices, cloud security architecture, SAST/DAST tooling, and security operations.

I thrive at the intersection of development velocity and security rigour: automating security gates in CI/CD pipelines so teams ship confidently, building dashboards that surface risk before it becomes an incident, and building organisational security culture from the ground up.
